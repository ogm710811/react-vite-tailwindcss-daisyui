Severity Level Quick Reference

General guidelines for incident classification

SEV-1 Critical

Complete service outage affecting all users. Revenue-impacting. Requires immediate all-hands response.

SEV-2 High

Major functionality degraded. Significant user impact. Requires rapid response within 30 minutes.

SEV-3 Medium

Partial degradation or workaround available. Limited user impact. Respond within business hours.

SEV-4 Low

Minor issue, cosmetic, or feature request. No immediate impact. Address in normal sprint cycle.
